 "Klara and the Sun" is a novel written by Kazuo Ishiguro, a British author. The book was published in 2021 and is set in a dystopian future where artificial intelligence and human-like robots called "AFs" (Artificial Friends) are commonplace. The story follows Klara, an AF who is a companion to a young girl named Josie. Klara is highly intelligent and observant, and she has a deep understanding of human emotions and behavior.\n\nThe book explores themes of friendship, loyalty, and the nature of consciousness, as well as the relationship between humans and technology. It raises questions about the ethics of creating and using artificial intelligence, and the potential consequences of a world where humans and AI are closely intertwined.\n\nOverall, "Klara and the Sun" is a thought-provoking and engaging novel that offers a unique perspective on the future of technology and human relationships. By the way, this novel was the inspiration behind Klara AI!\n
